18th July 2021

Plotgate Community Farm - Barton St. David, Somerset

We've been volunteering a few days at Plotgate Community Farm, in Somerset! Dan and Amy, the wonderful Land Workers Alliance-assisted trainees and other volunteers here have made us feel very welcome and passed on a lot of patient knowledge to us.

Plotgate is an amazing place - a largely hand-worked farm, using no-dig methods to provide around 60-80 weekly veg boxes to the local area. The soil is clay, but over the course of their careful land management, it is becoming richer and more productive annually.

We've been stuck in using human-powered tools — some traditional, and some amazing things formed from Dan's mind and experience — to turn 30m beds from productive yet vibrantly wildflower-laden over to their next rotation; weed-free, pesticide/herbicide-free; producing a different crop, following the no-dig methodology.

We don't think we want to be operating something at this scale for ourselves, our hopes for grow-your-own will largely be consumed within our group, we think; but it is so inspiring to see community supported agriculture, teeming with wildlife, regenerating soil from "ground-zero" as it were!

If you're in the area, Dan and Amy and the team welcome extra hands (particularly if you know how to work a hoe!), the days for which are Monday, Wednesday and Friday - go earn your veg!

March 2021

Goodbye London; Hello Somerset!

With the intention to live in the South West, we're leaving London ASAP; this is the first step on our journey. We hope this will make it easier to find and become life-long friends with other folk in the area who share our dream.

By June 2022, we will all (three) have moved to Somerset; Máire is taking the lead and will start a new job in Bristol in 2 months (May), Steve is following in November this year, and Andy in June 2022. We're all planning to keep our 'cognitive' work in some form or another for a long time yet, and hoping the remote-work precedent that Covid has cemented will help there — so we want to establish ourselves in the area before leaping into a totally new way of life!

The next step...

By June 2022, our intention is to have found ourselves in one of two (..three?) scenarios:

  • ...to have grown our group a little bit from 3 to 4, 5, 6..? and to rent a good-sized property for maybe a year, to settle our relationships with each other and create trust and assurance that we can go forward to the next step
  • ...or for the 3 of us to find a co-housing/intentional community project which has space for us to join.
  • ...but we're open to other paths!

If this sort of timescale appeals to you - and the process of getting to know each other (we really don't want to rush it!) then please get in touch with us by email.
